# Academic Research Skills for Claude Code

> Source: https://github.com/Imbad0202/academic-research-skills — 34,884 ⭐

## Core Purpose

ARS functions as an AI-augmented research assistant, not a replacement for human judgment. The system emphasizes human-in-the-loop workflows — researchers maintain control over research questions, methodology selection, and interpretation of findings.

## Four Integrated Skills

- **Deep Research**: Literature review, systematic reviews, and fact-checking with 13-agent teams
- **Academic Paper**: Writing support with 12 agents covering drafting, revision, and formatting
- **Academic Paper Reviewer**: Multi-perspective peer review with 7 agents including devil's advocate
- **Academic Pipeline**: 10-stage orchestration managing the full workflow with integrity checkpoints

## Notable Capabilities

- Citation verification across Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, and Crossref
- PRISMA-compliant systematic review support
- Style calibration that learns from your previous work
- Bilingual support (English and Traditional Chinese)
- Material Passport for cross-session resume capability
- Mandatory human checkpoints at Stages 2.5 and 4.5 (cannot be skipped)
